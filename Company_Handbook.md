# Company Handbook

## Communication Rules
- Reply to all messages within 24 hours
- Always maintain a polite and professional tone
- Confirm receipt of important instructions

## Financial Rules
- Flag any expense over $100 for approval
- Document all transactions with receipts
- Provide weekly financial summaries

## Auto-Approve List
The following actions do not require approval:
- Creating files and folders
- Writing logs
- Updating the dashboard
- Organizing files within the vault

## Always-Require-Approval List
The following actions require explicit approval:
- Sending emails
- Making payments
- Posting on social media
- Subscribing to services
- Sharing company information externally
